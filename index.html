Creative Direction

Do not just show the product. Build a reason to care.

Every creator has a unique audience, storytelling style, and creative niche. This section is designed to inspire creative thinking and help you naturally integrate POCOCO into your content. The examples are inspiration only, so adapt them based on your audience, expertise, and personal style.

01

Problem-Based Storytelling

For creators who educate, explain, or solve problems.

Start with a relatable challenge, misconception, or everyday pain point. Share your perspective, experience, or professional knowledge, then naturally introduce POCOCO as one part of a healthier environment or routine, not as the complete solution.

Suitable forSleep, Parenting, Mental Wellness, Neurodivergent Support, Educational Creators
02

Personal Experience Storytelling

For creators who share their own lifestyle and daily routines.

Invite your audience into your personal experiences, habits, and everyday moments. Rather than teaching, let POCOCO become a natural part of your story.

Suitable forWellness, Women's Lifestyle, Fitness, Productivity, Travel, Daily Vlogs
03

Immersive Experience Storytelling

For creators who inspire through visuals, atmosphere, and design.

Not every piece of content needs to solve a problem. Sometimes people connect with beautiful spaces, meaningful moments, and inspiring environments. Focus on lighting, atmosphere, interior design, aesthetics, and immersive storytelling.

Suitable forLifestyle, Home Decor, Interior Design, Cozy Living, Gift Ideas, Room Makeovers

Creator Success Checklist

Before it goes live, make it clear, useful, and approved.

These guidelines help you create authentic, engaging content while keeping the collaboration smooth. Create in your own style while keeping these recommendations in mind.

Filming Guidelines

How we would love the product to appear on camera.

  • Film in 1080P or higher
  • Show the product within the first 10 seconds
  • Capture multiple projection discs when available
  • Include product close-ups, projection details, and full-room atmosphere shots
  • Show the projection both with and without people in the scene
  • Use real lifestyle moments that fit your niche and audience
  • Keep the projection clear and in focus
  • Use stable footage
  • Avoid using a logo-only cover image

Product User Guide

Content Guidelines

What we would love your audience to experience.

  • Build the content around a clear point of view, not just product display
  • Connect POCOCO to a real audience need, mood, routine, or environment
  • Show how the projection changes the atmosphere before and after use
  • Let product benefits appear naturally through your story and experience
  • Match the scene, projection disc, and message to your audience
  • Bring the story back to your main theme before introducing a natural CTA
  • Keep the product visible for approximately 30%+ of the video

Creative Best Practices

Make the content feel human, not promotional.

We would love to see

  • People interacting with the projection
  • A cozy, immersive atmosphere
  • Natural storytelling in your own style
  • Everyday moments where POCOCO fits naturally
  • Authentic emotions and experiences

Please avoid

  • Unboxing-focused videos
  • Overly promotional language
  • Showing only a small projection area
  • Heavy filters or excessive visual effects
  • Unrealistic product claims

Publishing Requirements

Before your content goes live, please remember to:

  • Publish only after receiving final approval
  • Follow the approved concept and final edits
  • Include all required campaign details (affiliate link, discount code, hashtags, and official account tags)
